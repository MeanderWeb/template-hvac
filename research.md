- BBB A+ Rating (Coral AC, First Class, Plumbing Nerds)
- HVAC License number displayed prominently (CAC-prefixed FL license)
- Google review count front-and-center (2,100+ for Plumbing Nerds; 647 for Coral AC)
- Family-owned & operated messaging
- Women-owned badge (Air Necessity — strong differentiator)
- Non-commissioned technicians (Air Necessity — "no upselling")
- Financing available (Synchrony, Wells Fargo)
- Brand affiliations: Rheem, Ruud, Trane
- "Phones answered 24/7" — not just emergency service
